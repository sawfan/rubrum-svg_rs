Glyph sprite sheet (signs)

This renders the 12 zodiac sign symbols from the inlined sprite sheet via <use href="#rb-sign-aries">. The sprite is fetched from ./public/glyphs_white.svg and its <defs> are injected into the document so all glyphs can be referenced by fragment only.

Sign glyph comparison

Side-by-side: (1) raw <symbol> metadata from the sprite, (2) debug panel <use> rendering, (3) the same sign label <use> found inside the chart SVG. Rows can be sorted/highlighted by “raw rendered center delta” (how far the glyph’s content bbox center is from the middle of a 12×12 render box).
